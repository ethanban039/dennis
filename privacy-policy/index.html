Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026 · UK GDPR compliant

1. Introduction

Nottingham Kitchen Connect ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data when you use our website and services. We operate as a kitchen fitter matching service that connects Nottingham homeowners with vetted local kitchen fitters.

2. What Personal Data We Collect

When you submit our enquiry form, we collect the following personal data:

  • Full Name — so we can identify you and personalise your matching
  • Postcode — to identify your location and match you with local fitters in your area
  • Phone Number — so your matched fitter can contact you with your free quote
  • Budget Range — to help us match you with fitters who work within your budget
  • Timeline — to understand your project timeline and match appropriately

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data only for the following purposes:

  • Matching Service: To identify the best vetted local kitchen fitters in your area who match your project requirements
  • Communication: To share your contact details with our selected vetted partner fitters so they can contact you to discuss your project and provide a free 3D design and fixed quote
  • Improvement: To analyse service quality and improve our matching process (anonymised/aggregated data only)

We do not use your data for marketing purposes beyond your matched fitter contacting you about your kitchen enquiry.

4. Who We Share Your Data With

We share your personal data with our vetted partner kitchen fitters only. These are carefully selected local fitters who have been personally checked and approved to join our network. They receive your name, postcode, phone number, budget range, and timeline so they can contact you directly to provide a free 3D design and fixed quote.

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with anyone outside our vetted fitter network. We do not share your data with third-party marketing companies, advertisers, or data brokers.

5. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data under two legal bases under UK GDPR:

  • Legitimate Interest (Article 6(1)(f)): It is in our legitimate interest to match you with a suitable local fitter to provide you with a free service
  • Consent (Article 6(1)(a)): You explicitly agree to our privacy terms when you tick the GDPR consent checkbox on our form ("I agree to be contacted by a vetted local kitchen fitter about my enquiry")

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for 12 months following your enquiry. After this period, we securely delete your data. If a matched fitter is actively working with you, we may retain your data as needed for the duration of the project, plus 6 months for dispute resolution. You may request deletion at any time (see section 9).

7. Data Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption and access controls. We use HTTPS for all data transmission and only allow authorised personnel to access personal data. However, no transmission of data over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. If a security breach occurs, we will notify you as required by law.

8. Your Rights Under UK GDPR

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of Access: You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Rectification: You can correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to Erasure ('Right to Be Forgotten'): You can request we delete your data (subject to legal obligations)
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You can ask us to limit how we use your data
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request your data in a portable, machine-readable format
  • Right to Object: You can object to certain types of processing
  • Rights Related to Automated Decision-Making: You have rights if decisions are made purely by automated means

To exercise any of these rights, email us at mail@ethanbancroft.co.uk with "Data Subject Request" in the subject line. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Request Data Deletion

To request permanent deletion of your personal data, email mail@ethanbancroft.co.uk with the subject line "Data Deletion Request" and include:

  • Your full name
  • Your postcode
  • Your phone number

We will confirm deletion within 5 working days. Please note that deletion does not apply if a matched fitter is actively processing your project or if we have a legal obligation to retain your data (e.g., fraud prevention).

10. Cookies and Tracking

  • Analytics: Meta Pixel (Facebook) to track form submissions for advertising performance. You can opt out by adjusting your browser settings or using Meta's opt-out tools
